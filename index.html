What's This All About?

01.Make New Friends

One of the reasons we wanted to do this was to get to know new people, so even if you don't know anyone at the Pressler, please don't let that stop you from coming out! The more, the merrier.

02.Learn More About Your Community

Did you know that Karl is a tap dancer? Or that Dan can dance like there's no tomorrow? Join us as we uncover more hidden talents sprinkled throughout this lovely community of ours.

03.Have Fun!

Whether you're ready to step up to the spotlight or just here for the laughs, we're all about having a good time and making memories. In fact, we'll probably be doing just that all day before the event, so feel free to join us at the pool that afternoon!
